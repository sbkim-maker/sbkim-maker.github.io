Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 31, 2026

1. Overview

AstroArena Social Bot ("the Tool") is an internal marketing automation service operated by KRAFTON, Inc. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when the Tool interacts with TikTok platform APIs on behalf of KRAFTON-owned accounts.

2. Information We Collect

The Tool may collect or process the following types of information:

  • TikTok account identifiers (account IDs, usernames) for accounts owned and managed by KRAFTON.
  • Content performance metrics (views, likes, shares, completion rates) for videos published through the Tool.
  • Trending hashtag and sound data retrieved via TikTok Research API for internal content strategy purposes.
  • Access tokens required to authenticate API requests (stored securely, never shared externally).

3. How We Use the Information

All data collected by the Tool is used solely for KRAFTON's internal marketing operations, including:

  • Automating the publication of KRAFTON-owned gameplay video content to TikTok.
  • Analyzing content performance to optimize future marketing creatives.
  • Identifying trending topics in mobile gaming markets across target regions.

We do not sell, rent, or share collected data with third parties.

4. Data Retention

Performance metrics and trend data are retained for up to 12 months for internal reporting purposes. Access tokens are invalidated and removed upon revocation or expiration. Published video files are retained for 7 days after publication, then automatically deleted from local storage.

5. Data Security

The Tool operates on KRAFTON's internal infrastructure. API credentials are stored as environment variables and are never committed to version control or exposed publicly. Access is restricted to authorized KRAFTON marketing team members only.

6. Third-Party Services

The Tool interacts with the following third-party platforms via their official APIs: TikTok (ByteDance), Meta (Instagram), Google (Gemini AI). Each platform's own privacy policy governs their handling of data.

7. Your Rights

As this Tool processes only KRAFTON-owned account data and publicly available trend data, it does not process personal data of end users. If you have questions regarding data handled by this Tool, please contact us at the address below.
